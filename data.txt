Spend
Total ad cost per campaign/channel. This is the base for everything else.

Impressions
How many times ads were shown. Useful for reach and awareness.

Clicks
How many people clicked the ad.

CTR
Click-through rate: clicks / impressions.
Shows whether the creative/audience/message is attracting attention.

Conversions
The meaningful action: order placed, lead submitted, WhatsApp click, signup, etc.

Conversion Rate
conversions / clicks.
Shows how well traffic turns into actual results.

Cost Per Conversion / CPA
spend / conversions.
One of the most important performance metrics.

Revenue
Total sales/order value attributed to the campaign.

ROAS
Return on ad spend: revenue / spend.
For example, ROAS 4 means every $1 spent returned $4.

CAC or New Customer Cost
Cost to acquire a new customer specifically, not just any conversion. Very important if repeat customers are mixed with new ones.
